# 💸 finance-ai-agent

> *"Your broke era ends here."*

An AI-powered financial agent that tracks, analyzes, and advises your money —
all through natural conversation. No spreadsheets. No apps. Just talk.

---

## 😤 The Problem

Every month you wonder — *where did my salary go?*

- You open Excel → give up in 10 minutes
- You download a finance app → forget it in 3 days
- You make a budget → never follow it

**The real problem? Tracking money feels like work.**

---

## ✅ The Solution

`finance-ai-agent` changes that completely.

Just open your terminal and talk —
- *"I spent 300 on groceries"* → **Logged.**
- *"How much did I waste this week?"* → **Answered instantly.**
- *"Am I saving enough?"* → **Honest advice, right away.**

> *"Just talk to it — it logs your money, reads your patterns,*
> *and tells you the truth about your spending."*

---

## 🤖 What It Does

### 1️⃣ Listens & Logs
- Understands plain English — no commands to memorize
- Logs expenses and income instantly as you type
- Auto-detects categories: Food, Rent, Transport, Shopping & more

### 2️⃣ Thinks & Analyzes
- Organizes every transaction automatically
- Tracks spending daily, weekly, and monthly
- Spots patterns in your habits you didn't notice

### 3️⃣ Advises Like a CFO
- *"You spent 40% of your income on food this month"*
- *"You're on track to save 2000 this month"*
- *"Warning — overspending detected in Shopping"*

Real advice. Based on YOUR real data.

## 🛠️ How to Run

```bash
git clone https://github.com/yourusername/finance-ai-agent.git
cd finance-ai-agent
pip install -r requirements.txt
python main.py
```
